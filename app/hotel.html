CLUB ATLANTIS

Club Atlantis is located in Costa Adeje, in the Tenerife region of Canaries. Club Atlantis is in an excellent location situated in the Torviscas area of Playa de las Americas, next to Puerto Colon Marina. Club Atlantis is within easy reach of the resorts facilities and amenities, approximately 150 metres from the nearest beach and 100 metres from the bars, restaurants and nightlife. The 'duplex' bungalow has two bedrooms, one with a double bed and the other with 2 single beds. There is also a comfortable sofa-bed located in the lounge. There is a security safe available for guests to use. The fully equipped kitchen features a fridge-freezer, cooker, hob, microwave, toaster, kettle, cutlery and crockery. The lounge has a TV, and DVD player. There is a large upstairs patio area with a table and chairs and a large area downstairs. The family bathroom consists of a bath/shower, toilet and hand basin with dressing area. There is also a shower room equipped with toilet, hand basin and shower.
